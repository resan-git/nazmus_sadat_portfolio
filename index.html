Nazmus Sadat

Biomedical Engineering undergraduate focused on AI-driven medical image analysis. My work explores generative models to address skin tone bias in dermatological datasets.

Research Interests

  • Generative Adversarial Networks (Pix2Pix, CycleGAN)
  • Skin lesion image synthesis
  • Bias and fairness in medical AI
  • Skin tone representation in dermatology datasets
  • Interest in Large Language Models (LLMs) and RAG systems

Highlighted Work

I am currently working on my undergraduate thesis focused on generating skin lesion images with controlled Fitzpatrick skin tones using conditional generative models. The goal is to improve data diversity and reduce bias in dermatological AI systems.
